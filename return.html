How to Return Your Trial Order

S T Go is confident that you will love our products. However, if you are not completely satisfied with the products for any reason and would like to return your initial purchase (for a full refund up to $99) please review our terms and conditions and ensure you qualify. If yes then follow the directions below:

To return a Product for an exchange due to shipping damage or when cancelling your order during your 30 Day Trial Period, you will need to obtain a Return Merchandize Authorization ("RMA") number by contacting our Customer Service department at 1-310-274-5141 or email us online at info@stgo.com.

An RMA number can ONLY be obtained by contacting the Customer Service Department by phone or by email.


Please note:

1. We cannot process or exchange Product marked "Return to Sender."

2. To ensure that your account is correctly noted, you must send back Product returns to the address provided below along with your RMA number, your full name, billing address and your phone number. The RMA number must be clearly written on the package that you are sending back. Our shipping department is NOT allowed to accept any packages without an RMA number.
